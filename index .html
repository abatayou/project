About Shadowing

Shadowing - is a language learning technique where you listen to native speakers and try to repeat what they say as closely as possible, mimicking their pronunciation, intonation, and rhythm. This method helps improve your speaking skills, accent, and overall fluency by training your ear and mouth to work together in producing natural-sounding speech.
